    Data Sources
    • NOAA / National Weather Service: local forecasts, alerts, and hourly conditions.
    • NOAA NESDIS GOES imagery: full-disk Earth satellite image tile.
    • USNO Astronomical Applications: sun and moon timing data.
    • NASA Scientific Visualization Studio (SVS) Dataset 5587 (https://svs.gsfc.nasa.gov/5587/): moon phase imagery by UTC hour-of-year frame.
    • Open-Meteo: UV and supplemental environmental data.
